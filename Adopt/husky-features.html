Features

One of their most beloved features, Huskies are known for their distinctive-looking almond-shaped eyes, which can be seen in a variety of colors. They are most commonly seen with either ice blue eyes or deep brown eyes, but sometimes even this varies.

 

Some Huskies have one eye of each color (bi-eyed), or more rarely, eyes that have a mixture of both blue and brown in them (known as parti-colored). While there are other dog breeds that also have a multi-hued range of eye colors (like Border Collies and Australian Shepherds), Huskies are most commonly known for this phenomenon.

Both blue and brown eyes are normal for Huskies and rarely represent any eye problems or illnesses.
