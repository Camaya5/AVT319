Christian Rodriguez


Having studied at Savannah College of Art and Design, born and raised in New York City
by immigrants of the Dominican Republic, Christian spent his
childhood traveling between the two islands and cultures.
His photographic work focuses on issues of immigration,
cultural identity, and the Dominican diaspora.
